How to Use:
For the Semester GPA Calculator:
  1. Fill in your classes --they should autofill and add the proper grading type (if it doesn't reach out to me to fix it)
  2. Put in your grades for each class with the dropdown then hit "Get GPA"
  3. Congrats! Those are your two GPAs-- your weighted and your unweighted
